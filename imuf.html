IMU-F

Top hat and monocle level of sophistication for IMU sensors

Ways to transmit clear signals the mostly complete list:
  • Speech
  • Pay a courier
  • Morse code
  • Smoke signals
  • Expensive trained pigeons
  • Body language

  • Electrical communication from a programmable IMU filtered by recursive least square methods based on time series analysis

We are using the last option on the list. You wouldn’t believe how many of our competitors are using a different option for flight data. The answer all of them. Our IMU-F  is a breakthrough in chip communication. The added power to the board is solely dedicated to filtering junk data into a pure jetstream of clean flight data. Noise is a huge problem for flight. The  IMU-F  is specifically built to break, tackle, and crack noise trends to provide an unparalleled flight experience.

Things IMU-F  filters:
  • Wind noise
  • Vibration noise
  • Prop wash
  • Mostother types of unpredictable noise
  • Sound of Mother-in-law's voice

(Occasionally it does not filterstray cosmic raysonly direct cosmic rays. We are researching whether it is possible to filter those without multiple redundant CPUs and sensors. I think you get the point though. IMU-F  filters noise really well.)

Things we think IMU-F  may not filter:
  • Coffee
